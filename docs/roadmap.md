# Product Roadmap

This roadmap advances Brunel from a reliable software foundation to an evidence-grounded construction operating system.

The roadmap is capability-driven. Dates and priorities will be validated with construction users before implementation.

## Foundation — current

- Stable module boundaries and typed contracts
- Configuration, logging, composition, and dependency injection
- Citation and source identity primitives
- Testable extension points for model providers and agents
- Architecture and product guardrails

## Project memory and document intelligence

- Controlled document intake, versioning, metadata, and permissions
- Parsing for drawings, specifications, RFIs, submittals, meeting minutes, and reports
- Citation-aware indexing and retrieval
- Evaluation datasets for retrieval quality and source fidelity

## Construction workflows

- Construction QA and cited cross-document questions
- RFI, submittal, meeting-action, and daily-report administration
- Drawing and specification intelligence
- Schedule and procurement intelligence
- Risk signals with evidence, confidence, and human disposition

## Specialized operations

- Cost and change-management support
- Safety documentation support with strict human authority boundaries
- Commissioning readiness, issue tracking, and turnover
- Portfolio-level owner visibility

## Platform maturity

- Local and hosted deployment options
- Provider-neutral local and OpenAI model adapters
- Role-aware access, audit trails, observability, and evaluation
- Integration adapters selected by customer need

Every phase must include security review, construction-domain validation, grounded-output evaluation, and a clear rollback path.
