# Roadmap

1. **Lesson 1 — Organization and Responsibility Registry (current):** participants, relationships, responsibility routing, sources, UI, and API.
2. Document register with source, author, version, and date metadata.
3. RFI and submittal workflows with human review gates.
4. Schedule and procurement tracking.
5. Quality, commissioning, punch-list, and turnover workflows.
6. Carefully evaluated AI assistance grounded in traceable project records.

Each feature will be driven by a Construction MBA lesson. Authentication, cloud deployment, and LLM features are intentionally deferred.
